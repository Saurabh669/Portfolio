Grandma's Secreat Apple Pie

The apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fail to delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-size, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Pre heat over to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the cinnamon and sugar.
  3. Toss the apple in the sugar-cinnanon mixture.
